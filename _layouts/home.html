PhD Candidate in Artificial Intelligence

Alex Pierron

Safe reinforcement learning for critical cyber-physical systems.

I am a PhD candidate in Mathematics and Artificial Intelligence at Institut Polytechnique de Paris, within the SAMOVAR laboratory. My research focuses on Physical AI, reinforcement learning and the security of B5G and 6G physical-layer systems.

I study how mono-agent and multi-agent RL methods can remain efficient, robust and trustworthy when they act on critical physical systems. My background in mathematics and fundamental physics shapes how I connect models, algorithms and deployment constraints.

  • Multi-Agent RL
  • Cyber-Physical Security
  • B5G/6G
