About me

I am a PhD candidate in Mathematics and Artificial Intelligence at Institut Polytechnique de Paris , within the SAMOVAR laboratory , under the supervision of Joaquin Garcia-Alfaro . My research lies at the intersection of Physical AI, reinforcement learning, and the physical-layer security of next-generation communication networks.

I am particularly interested in developing intelligent systems that remain efficient, robust, and trustworthy when deployed in critical physical environments.

My background in mathematics and physics shapes the way I approach research, connecting theoretical models, learning algorithms, and real-world deployment constraints. Alongside my academic work, I also explore software engineering through personal projects.

I enjoy learning across disciplines and sharing what I discover along the way. I believe that some of the most valuable advances in AI emerge from building meaningful connections between different fields.

Feel free to explore my work and get in touch !
