About me

I am a PhD candidate in Mathematics and Artificial Intelligence at Institut Polytechnique de Paris within SAMOVAR laboratory under the supervision of Joaquin Garcia-Alfaro. My research lies at the intersection of Physical AI, Reinforcement Learning, and the Physical-Layer Security of next-generation 6G communication networks.
My thesis is part of the PEPR Future Networks program, a French strategic and large-scale research initiative under France 2030 , dedicated to advancing next-generation communication technologies.

My background in mathematics and physics shapes the way I approach research, connecting theoretical models, learning algorithms, and real-world deployment constraints.
I am particularly interested in developing intelligent systems that remain efficient, robust, and trustworthy when deployed for critical Cyber-Physical Systems in realistic physical environments.

I enjoy learning across disciplines and sharing what I discover along the way. I believe that some of the most valuable advances in AI emerge from building meaningful connections between different fields ! Alongside my academic work, I also explore software engineering through personal projects.

Feel free to reach out ! I am always happy to discuss research, collaborations, or simply exchange ideas.
